Groups are the coloured quick-tap buttons on the cashier screen. A weighed group (e.g. Rice) opens a variety list then the weight pad; a fixed-price group (e.g. LPG, Oil) opens a list you tap to add straight to the bill. A group's button appears once it has at least one product — assign products to a group on the product's Edit page.
